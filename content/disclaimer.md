### Disclaimer

Yoruba is a language of many dialects, and the interpretation of any
proverb varies by region, generation, and context of use. The archive
records these variants rather than adjudicating between them.

Entries are compiled with care. Errors and omissions are inevitable
in a living corpus. Corrections are welcomed at
`hello@oweyoruba.org`.
