### Data Sources

This page lists every source the archive has drawn on.

**Wikiquote, Yoruba proverbs.** Wikiquote's Yoruba corpus is licensed
CC BY-SA. Òwe Yòòbá uses Yoruba text from Wikiquote and writes all
English translations independently, so that the archive's own corpus
can remain uniformly CC BY 4.0. Wikiquote is cited in
`source_attribution` for the entries where it was used.

**Personal and elder contributions.** Named contributors are credited
by display name. Family and community elders in the Yoruba-speaking
regions of southwestern Nigeria have provided both text and usage
guidance.

**Print references (see-also citations only).** Delano, *Owe L'esin
Oro* (1966). Ajibola, *Owe Yoruba* (1947). Bamgbose, various.
Sheba, various. These works are cited by number where a compiled entry
overlaps, following normal scholarly practice.

**Reference-only, pending permission.** Owomoyela, *The Good Person*
(2005). yoruba.unl.edu, University of Nebraska-Lincoln Center for
Digital Research in the Humanities. Both are cited but not reproduced
pending outreach.
