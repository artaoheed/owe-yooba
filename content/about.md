### About

Òwe Yòòbá is a living archive of Yoruba proverbs, compiled and
maintained by Taoheed A. Abdulraheem. The project is standalone,
non-commercial, and published under Creative Commons Attribution 4.0
(CC BY 4.0) for content and MIT for code.

Contact: `hello@oweyoruba.org`.
