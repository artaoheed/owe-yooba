### Corrections

Send corrections to `hello@oweyoruba.org` with a subject line
beginning `[correction]` followed by the entry slug. Include the
original text, the proposed correction, and a source or reasoning
where possible.

Accepted corrections are applied as git commits to the public
repository. The full history of every entry is therefore public and
auditable.
